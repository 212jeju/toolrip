Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Toolrip (toolrip.com). Toolrip is a platform offering 100+ free online utility tools including converters, calculators, text processors, developer tools, and more. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle information when you use our services.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and what choices you have when you visit and use Toolrip. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Toolrip does not collect personal data. We do not require account creation, registration, or login to use any of our tools. You can use every tool on Toolrip without providing your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

All of our tools operate entirely within your web browser using client-side processing. The data you enter into our tools (such as text, numbers, files, or images) is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

We do not store, log, or have access to any content you input into our tools.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

While Toolrip itself does not set cookies for tracking purposes, some of the third-party services integrated into our website may use cookies and similar technologies. Below is a detailed explanation of each service:

3.1 Google AdSense (Third-Party Advertising Cookies)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet. These cookies allow Google and its partners to deliver advertising that may be relevant to your interests.

The information collected by Google AdSense cookies typically includes your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and timestamps. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3.2 Google Analytics (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our website. GA4 collects information such as pages visited, time spent on pages, referring websites, and general geographic location (country or city level). GA4 uses first-party cookies to distinguish unique users and track sessions.

Google Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized. We use this information solely to improve our website and understand which tools are most useful to our visitors. You can learn more about how Google uses data at How Google uses information from sites that use its services.

3.3 Cloudflare Web Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics service. Unlike traditional analytics platforms, Cloudflare Web Analytics does not use cookies and does not track individual users. It collects only aggregated, non-personally-identifiable metrics such as page views, referral sources, and general browser information. No personal data is collected or stored through this service.

4. Third-Party Advertising

Toolrip displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history. Personalized advertising helps support our ability to provide free tools to users worldwide.

How to opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Google Ad Settings: Visit Google Ad Settings to control the ads Google shows you and to opt out of personalized advertising.
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): Visit the NAI opt-out page to opt out of targeted advertising from NAI member companies.
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): Visit the DAA opt-out page to manage your advertising preferences.
  • European users: Visit Your Online Choices to manage ad personalization from participating companies in Europe.

Please note that opting out of personalized advertising does not mean you will stop seeing ads on Toolrip. You will continue to receive non-personalized advertisements.

5. Data Processing

All tool processing happens locally in your web browser. When you use a Toolrip tool, such as converting a file, formatting text, or performing a calculation, the processing occurs entirely on your device using JavaScript. No data is uploaded to or processed on our servers.

This means:

  • Your files, text, images, and other inputs never leave your device.
  • We have no ability to view, access, or recover any data you process using our tools.
  • Once you close or refresh the browser tab, any data in the tool is permanently gone.
  • No server-side logs of your tool usage or input data are created.

6. Children's Privacy

Toolrip is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since Toolrip does not collect personal data from any user, we believe our service is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at privacy@toolrip.com, and we will take appropriate steps to address the concern.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and related data protection laws. Although Toolrip does not collect personal data directly, third-party services on our site (such as Google AdSense and Google Analytics) may process some data. Your rights include:

  • Right of Access: You have the right to request information about what personal data, if any, is processed in connection with your use of our website.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable legal obligations.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes, including profiling for such purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@toolrip.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

The legal basis for processing data through third-party cookies on our website is your consent, which you can withdraw at any time by adjusting your browser cookie settings or using the opt-out links listed in Section 4 above.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request that we delete any personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. Toolrip does not sell personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

Since Toolrip does not directly collect personal information and does not sell personal data, most CCPA provisions are inherently satisfied. If you have any questions or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@toolrip.com.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Your continued use of Toolrip after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

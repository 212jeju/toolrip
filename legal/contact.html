Before You Contact Us

Check if your question is answered below. This helps us respond faster to unique inquiries.

Is Toolrip free to use?

Yes, all tools on Toolrip are completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no sign-up required. Our platform is supported by advertising, which allows us to keep every tool accessible to everyone at no cost. We believe that essential online utilities should be available to all users regardless of their budget.

Do you store my data?

No. Privacy is a core principle at Toolrip. All data processing happens directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript. We do not upload, store, or transmit your input data to any server. Once you close the browser tab, your data is gone. We do not use cookies for tracking, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information through our tools.

Can I suggest a new tool?

Absolutely! We are always looking for ideas to expand our collection of free utilities. If there is a tool you wish existed or one you use frequently that could be improved, please let us know through the contact form above. Select "Feature Request" as the subject and describe the tool you have in mind. We review every suggestion and prioritize based on community demand.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

We appreciate you taking the time to report issues. Please use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Include the following details to help us fix it quickly: the name of the tool, a description of what went wrong, the steps to reproduce the issue, and your browser name and version (for example, Chrome 120, Firefox 121, or Safari 17). Screenshots are also helpful if you can attach them via email.
